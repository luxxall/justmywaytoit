22th December 2022

SPOJ, C++

SPOJ
Stopped my game development
I discovered a website SPOJ which contains exercises for programmer to test their skills. Tried to do first task but I failed. I had problem with understanding meaning of the problem in that case. Stopped to develop the game. I think it’s pointless when I’m looking for some code on the Internet to do particular thing in my game and then I just rewrite it. I want to learn to write those elements by myself and to understand of how its made. I really want to be a part of IT and to do that I have to learn and learn and learn to be good at programming, to understand what I’m doing, to have knowledge, to have a portfolio, make some apps. I really enjoy coding and I hope not to loose this fire.
