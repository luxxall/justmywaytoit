Finally after some troubles I made program called substitution for CS50. Beginnings were really tough. Surprisingly I didn’t have much problems with writing the code but thing that was holding me was to work in organised way. I didn’t write down step by step how to make my program to work, instead I just started writing it but I get frustrated every time it didn’t work. After that I checked on CS50 page what’s is TODO in which order and that made my work organised I was able to finish the program. This shows me that I must pay bigger attention to think about how to work, how to organise my work and maybe write down milestones for my program and steps that should be made to complete the task. Next time I will take more time to think about the project and make a TODO list.
